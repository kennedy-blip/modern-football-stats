How to Use Football Daily

📅 Selecting Matches

1. Choose a date using the date picker or click "Today's Matches"

2. Optionally filter by league using the league dropdown

3. Click "Search Matches" to load the data

👥 Viewing Lineups

1. Click "View Details" on any match card

2. Go to the "Lineups" tab to see starting players and substitutes

3. Each player shows their number and position

📊 Match Statistics

1. In match details modal, click the "Stats" tab

2. View comprehensive match statistics like shots, possession, etc.

3. Compare performance between home and away teams

🎯 Predictions

1. Click the "Predictions" tab at the top

2. View AI-calculated probabilities for match outcomes

3. Predictions include: Win probabilities, Over/Under goals, Both Teams to Score

💡 Tips

• The data is powered by the free tier of APIfootball.com

• Free tier includes England Championship and France Ligue 2

• Predictions are calculated using advanced algorithms

• Match details update in real-time for live matches
